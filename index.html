This web application provides an overview of the monthly and yearly precipitation data in the Netherlands. On the left side of the page a map of the Netherlands is visualised, indicating all the Dutch 325 on land weather stations on default. By selecting the first dropdown menu one is able to choose to visualise either all weather stations or those maintained by the KNMI. By hovering over the weather stations you are able to obtain the location and further information is displayed in the box below. On the right side graph 1 shows data of the 5 selected weather stations in the map, graph 2 visualises data of the first selected station over the past 4 years. In the second dropdown menu the year of the data visualised in graph 1 can be chosen.
